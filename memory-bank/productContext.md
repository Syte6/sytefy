# Product Context

- **Problem**: Small businesses need a unified assistant to manage customers, appointments, finances, and reminders without juggling multiple tools.
- **Solution**: Sytefy provides a SaaS console (Next.js) backed by a secure FastAPI service with modular features so teams can onboard gradually.
- **User Goals**:
  - Seamless onboarding (register, setup business profile, invite staff).
  - Reliable reminders/notifications to reduce no-shows.
  - Quick insight into customers and finances from a single dashboard.
- **UX Principles**: Minimal clicks to reach data, dark-mode friendly, localized Turkish copy, consistent CTA placement, and optimistic updates with fallbacks.
